# Frequently Asked Questions

## Shipping times
Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days within the continental US.
Expedited shipping takes 2-3 business days and costs an additional $12.99.
International orders take 10-21 business days depending on destination and
customs processing. We do not offer same-day delivery at this time.

## Order tracking
Every order receives a tracking link by email within 24 hours of the order
shipping (not the same as order placement - processing takes 1-2 business
days before an order ships). If a tracking link shows "label created" for
more than 3 business days with no movement, that means the package has not
yet been picked up by the carrier and the customer should contact support.

## Return policy
Items can be returned within 30 days of delivery for a full refund, provided
they are unused and in original packaging. Final sale items (marked as such
on the product page) cannot be returned. Return shipping is free for
defective items; for other returns, a $6.99 return label fee is deducted
from the refund unless the customer used our free returns portal within 14
days of delivery.

## Refund processing time
Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5-10 business days
of us receiving the returned item. Store credit refunds (chosen at checkout)
are issued instantly upon return confirmation.

## Password resets
Customers can reset their password from the login page using "Forgot
password." Reset links expire after 1 hour. If a customer says the reset
email never arrived, ask them to check spam and confirm the email on file
matches their account before escalating to engineering.

## Order cancellation
Orders can be cancelled for free within 1 hour of placement, before they
enter processing. After that window, the order cannot be cancelled but can
be returned once delivered, per the standard return policy.

## Damaged or missing items
If an order arrives damaged or missing items, customers should not be asked
to return the damaged item at their own cost. Support should offer a free
replacement or full refund, and damaged-item claims do not require the
$6.99 return label fee.

## Gift cards
Gift cards do not expire and are non-refundable for cash, but remaining
balances can be checked at any time on the account page. Gift cards cannot
be used to purchase other gift cards.
